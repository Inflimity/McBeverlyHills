Our Story

McBeverly Hills Consulting Limited

MCBEVERLY HILLS CONSULTING LIMITED is a limited liability Company registered with Nigeria’s Corporate Affairs Commission on the 15th Day of November, 2011 as a Learning and Development Organization. It is a proudly Nigerian Company with a global outreach. While ensuring International Best Practices in all its activities and processes, it is deeply involved in the Capacity Building of the contemporary workforce across numerous Industries and Sectors of not only the Nigerian Economy but also at the global stage from the point of entry to departure drawing upon its highly cerebral and experienced Professionals and versatile Resource Persons. MCBEVERLY HILLS Consulting is all about “People and Processes” with the overall objective of creating a positive work environment for its ever-expanding client base by maintaining employee motivation and implementing strategies for organizational growth and success. Without mincing words, MCBEVERLY HILLS CONSULTING LIMITED helps interested organizations from both the Public and Private Sectors to optimize their most valuable asset: their people. Our tailor-made services include Corporate Transformation, Change Management, Strategy/Management and Training by designing and implementing Courses and Workshops and preparing the Organizations themselves for new processes, technologies or Organizational changes.
